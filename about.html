Who am I?

Early in my career as a designer, I got really pissed off tired of been only requested to make pretty layouts. Determined to change that, I engaged in a mission to speak the language of business leaders and show how a Design-oriented mindset dramatically transforms the way organizations manage decisions, products, and teams.‍
