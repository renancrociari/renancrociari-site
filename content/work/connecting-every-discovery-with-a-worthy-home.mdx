## The Problem

Researchers spend countless hours trying to find the right journal for their manuscripts. The process is often frustrating, time-consuming, and leads to suboptimal publication choices.

## Our Solution

We created a **Journal Finder** tool that intelligently matches research papers with suitable journals based on:
- Subject matter alignment
- Impact factor preferences
- Open access requirements
- Publication timelines

## Design Process

### Discovery
- 30+ interviews with researchers
- Analysis of 10,000+ journal searches
- Competitive landscape review

### Iteration
- 5 rounds of usability testing
- A/B testing of matching algorithms
- Continuous feedback integration

## Outcomes

- **3x** faster journal selection
- **85%** user satisfaction rate
- **50%** reduction in inappropriate submissions
- Featured in 5 major research institutions

## Recognition

This project was selected for internal design award at Springer Nature and has become a core feature of the researcher platform.
