## Context

Farfetch is a leading global platform for luxury fashion, connecting customers in over 190 countries with items from more than 700 boutiques and brands. As a Senior Product Designer, I focused on improving performance for the top-performing boutiques on the platform.

## Goals

- Increase conversion rates for boutique partners
- Reduce friction in the shopping experience
- Improve page load performance
- Enhance mobile experience

## Approach

Through extensive user research and data analysis, we identified key pain points in the customer journey. We redesigned critical touchpoints while maintaining the luxury aesthetic expected by our users.

## Results

- **+15%** increase in conversion rate
- **-20%** reduction in page load time
- **+25%** improvement in mobile engagement
- **95%** boutique satisfaction score

## Key Learnings

Working with luxury fashion taught me the delicate balance between performance optimization and maintaining brand prestige. Every millisecond matters, but so does every pixel.
