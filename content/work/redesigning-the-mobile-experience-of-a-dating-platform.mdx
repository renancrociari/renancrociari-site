## Challenge

Modern dating apps often prioritize quantity over quality. Our goal was to create an experience that fostered **meaningful connections** while prioritizing **user safety** and **mental well-being**.

## Research Insights

Through user interviews and behavioral analysis, we discovered:
- Users felt overwhelmed by endless swiping
- Safety concerns were a major barrier to engagement
- Profile authenticity was a key trust factor

## Solution

We introduced a redesigned experience with:
- **Curated matches** based on shared values
- **Enhanced safety features** including video verification
- **Conversation starters** to reduce awkwardness
- **Wellness features** to prevent burnout

## Impact

- **40%** increase in meaningful conversations
- **60%** reduction in reported safety incidents
- **4.5★** app store rating (up from 3.8★)
- **2M+** new active users in first 6 months
